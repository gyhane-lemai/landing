Our goal

Helping students to feel a little safer in every situation and at every moments, our aim is security and serenity for everyone. Go home safely with our application!

The concept

A map that evolve in real-time to help you take the good way home.

Travel buddies, who can go with you if you have a path in common.

Alerts to your family and the police in case of emergency or if you are not responding
